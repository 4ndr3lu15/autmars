What is ATUMARS?

AUTMARS is a platform for the production of images generated by machine learning techiniques from text. The sole purpose of the website is to provide a unique experience for the user to exercise their creative power in any way that suits them.

How it works?

After creating your account, you can click on the button “create” and then you will go to a page where you see a text box. Insert a text, choose your parameters, wait some minutes and Voilà! Enjoy your art made with AI.
